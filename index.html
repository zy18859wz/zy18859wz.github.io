Weizhe Niu

Low-dimensional topology, symmetries of manifolds, and embedding problems.

About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Yau Mathematical Sciences Center, Tsinghua University. Previously, I completed my PhD in Mathematics at the University of Glasgow under the supervision of Brendan Owens and Mark Powell. Before that, I studied at the University of Warwick and the University of Nottingham.

My research interests are in low-dimensional topology, especially questions related to symmetries of manifolds and embedding problems.
